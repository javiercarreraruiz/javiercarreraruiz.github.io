solutions

Cloud Consulting and Migration Services: We provide consulting and advisory services on how you can best use the cloud to make your applications elastic, reliable and efficient. We can also analyze your current infrastructure and help you effortlessly migrate applications to the cloud.

 

DevOps Automation: We are experts in configuring servers with Configuration Management tools, helping you to automate the process of deploying and scaling applications in a managed, consistent and repeatible infrastructure.

 

Cloud Application Development: We architect and develop cloud-ready mobile and web applications, leveraging the Cloud's features to deliver the best performance, resilience and scalability.

 

Web-Scale Converged Infrastructure: Web-scale is a transformative approach to buying, deploying and managing infrastructure. Pioneered by Internet companies, it is now available to enterprises thanks to market leader and visionary Nutanix.

Technologies

Multicloud and datacenter convergence: In a competitive and rapidly evolving market, you need a partner with skills in a variety of cloud options, both public, private and hybrid. Our expertise areas include Amazon Web Services (AWS), Windows Azure, Google Cloud Platform, Openstack, VMware and Nutanix.

 

DevOps Tools: Modern hybrid cloud environmnets and lean/agile development and operational practices require new tools. We use and integrate Puppet, Docker, Vagrant, Chef and Ansible in your stack.

 

Application Development: We develop fault-tolerant, scalable cloud-ready mobile and web application using iOS, Android, JavaScript, Java, Python, Django, Ruby and Node.js.
