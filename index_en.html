VISION

Antelops  is a Cloud Computing and DevOps Automation Consulting and Training company. We specialize in designing, implementing and supporting highly-scalable and self-healing applications powered by the leading Cloud and Automation solutions.

solutions

Cloud Consulting and Migration Services: We provide consulting and advisory services on how you can best use the cloud to make your applications elastic, reliable and efficient. We can also analyze your current infrastructure and help you effortlessly migrate applications to the cloud. We have experts in Amazon Web Services (AWS), Windows Azure, Google Cloud Platform, Eucalyptus, Openstack and VMware.

 

DevOps Automation: We are experts in configuring servers with Configuration Management tools, helping you to automate the process of deploying and scaling applications in a managed, consistent and repeatible infrastructure. We have experts in Puppet, Chef, Ansible and Saltstack.

 

Cloud Application Development: We architect and develop cloud-ready mobile and web applications, leveraging the Cloud's features to deliver the best performance, resilience and scalability. We have experts in iOS, Android, JavaScript, Java, Python, Django, Ruby and Node.js.

training
